# Assassin's Creed Odyssey
<img width="276" height="361" alt="ACOdysseyCoverArt" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/34d86682-c319-4222-adab-8c8a79311225" />

**Release Date:** October 5, 2018

**Platforms:**
  - Playstation 4
  - Xbox One
  - Windows
  - Nintendo Switch
  - Stadia

**Setting:** During a mythological history of the Peloponnesian War between Athens and Sparta from 431 to 422 BCE. The game includes locations such as Athens, Sparta, Lesbos, Macedonia, Delphi, Phokis, and many others. 

**Player Characters:**
  -  Kassandra or Alexios (Assassin ancestor)
  -  Layla Hassan (descendant)
