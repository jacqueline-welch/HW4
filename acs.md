**Release Date:** October 23, 2015

**Platforms:**
  - Playstation 4
  - Xbox One
  - Windows
  - Stadia

**Setting:** London, England, in 1868, at the onset of the Second Industrial Revolution. There is also a segment set in 1916 during World War I, where the player plays as Lydia Frye (Jacob's granddaughter).

**Player Characters:**
  -  Jacob and Evie Frye
  -  Lydia Frye
  -  "The Initiate" / [Helix](helix.md) player
