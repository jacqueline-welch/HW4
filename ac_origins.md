# Assassin's Creed Origins

**Release Date:** October 27, 2017
***
**Platforms:**
  - Playstation 4
  - Xbox One
  - Windows
  - Stadia

**Setting:** Egypt, near the end of the Ptolemaic period from 49 to 38 BCE, including the locations of Alexandria, Memphis, the Giza Pyramid Complex, the Nile River and its delta, the Siwa Oasis, and Heracleion.

**Player Characters:**
  -  Bayek of Siwa (Assassin ancestor)
  -  Layla Hassan (descendant)
